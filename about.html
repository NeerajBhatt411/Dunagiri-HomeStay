About Panchvati Hills Homestay

Panchvati Hills Homestay began as a small family initiative to welcome travelers seeking peace and spiritual connection. Located near the sacred Mahavatar Babaji Cave in Dunagiri, our homestay blends traditional Kumaoni hospitality with comfortable modern amenities.

We are a family-run homestay — hosts who love cooking, guiding guests to nearby attractions, and sharing local stories. Our goal: give guests a restful, authentic mountain experience.

  • • Family-run homestay with personalized care
  • • Emphasis on cleanliness, comfort and local cuisine
  • • Help with local travel, guides and pickup on request

Our Mission

To create a quiet, caring space where travelers can unplug, experience local culture and find inner calm in nature’s arms.

Our Promise

Clean rooms, honest hospitality, warm home-cooked food and local guidance. We welcome people seeking spirituality, nature or a peaceful family getaway.
